    Submit A Work Order

    The Association is excited to share our new electronic work order system with you.

    Starting November 4, 2024, owners will be able to place work orders online!

    The work orders are for repairs at your unit at common areas/responsibilities such as :

    • screen repair & replacements
    • front door maintenance & repair
    • window maintenance & repair
    • glass sliding door maintenance & repair
    • patio fence maintenance & repair
    • outside siding maintenance & repair

    ***Please continue to contact Maintenance directly at 210-464-5434 if you have an active leak!
    We will be able to react quickly and avoid possible damage to your unit and other areas.***


    Here's how to place a work order online:

    1. Click the yellow "Submit a Work Order" button below.
    2. Fill out the required fields and submit the form.
    3. We will notify you when the work order was accepted and completed.
