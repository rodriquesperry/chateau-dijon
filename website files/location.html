    • Chateau Dijon Townhomes

    Chateau Dijon Condominiums is comprised of three different buildings, each with its own unique personality, attributes and advantages. 7707 Broadway boasts two New Orleans style courtyards that invite you to sit down and relax on the busiestof days, charming second story walkways and crossover bridges, a number of the brick- fronted units facing Broadway – one of the most exciting streets in San Antonio replete with many of our best bookshops, coffee shops, antique stores, restaurants and vintage clothing shops – as well its own pool which is set in a garden-like area.
    7709 Broadway is our Grand Hotel look alike in the best European tradition. It is a three-story building fronted by a beautiful fountain and back-ended by the St. Anthony de Padua Catholic Church and grounds which ensure a spacious vista over an area that will never be further developed. For those who prefer a more traditional apartment style building, but one situated in a green and open area, this is a rare find in one of America’s fastest growing cities.
    7711 Broadway also boasts brick-fronted units right on Broadway, the second story walkways and bridges but many other units are situated along winding paths behind the pool. Many of these units are two-story townhomes with baluster adorned windows facing out on three sides. The old world feel is inescapable since the magic of this particular building is the way light and shadow fall across an area that could have easily provided the setting for one of Van Gogh’s village landscapes. This is the building where it is easiest to remember that no two of our units are ever alike.
